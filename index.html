What

What I do

I specialize in helping smaller companies and tech startups create and implement pricing strategies that reflect value of their unique products, correspond to the product strategy, strongly contribute to achieving their business goals and help differentiate from competition. Whether it's software licenses, SaaS, PaaS, IaaS, or any other pricing model, I've got you covered — I've worked with them all.

My services include consulting and advisory with the focus on formulating pricing strategies, defining packaging and pricing principles, and setting price points. In working with my clients, we're going through the full "pricing decision chain":

Pricing strategy → Packaging → Charging model → Pricing metric → Price tiering → Price points → Price localization

Why

Why you should work with me

You are a growing company in need of pricing help. You understand pricing is important, but don't know how to get it right or what you're missing. Hiring someone full-time is an overkill for you and engaging big consulting firms will leave a hole in your budget and take forever to complete. Sounds familiar?

The tech market is constantly changing, and staying ahead of the curve is crucial for your pricing strategy.
